## Selection rule

The final original-passage layer is selected deterministically.

For each of 12 works, candidate passages are filtered to keep candidates that:

- passed Step 7 length/sentence checks;
- contain 450–650 words;
- contain at least 5 sentences;
- are not from the first five candidate windows of the work;
- do not contain front-matter or boilerplate indicators such as Project Gutenberg markers, contents pages, illustration blocks, or preface material.

From each work, 30 candidates are selected by evenly spreading selection across the remaining candidate sequence.
